NPC Overview

Kimiko Nomura is a behind-the-scenes specialist who supports active hunters through research, logistics, and recovery. Operating from the Occultarium’s expert lounge, she serves as the intellectual backbone of the team—connecting field reports, ancient lore, and real-time analysis into actionable intelligence.

Profile & Operational Record

Appearance & Demeanor

Kimiko appears reserved and professional, often dressed in comfortable academic attire suited for long hours of study. She carries herself with quiet confidence, speaking precisely and thoughtfully. While not confrontational, she remains steady under pressure and rarely panics—even when surrounded by the supernatural.

Operational History

A lifelong academic prodigy, Kimiko gravitated toward the Occultarium during her studies, drawn by its unparalleled supernatural archives. A near-fatal vampire attack marked her first direct encounter with the hidden world. Rescued by Anasi Banyan IV and Angela, she was later recruited—not as a hunter, but as the team’s primary research and logistical support specialist.
