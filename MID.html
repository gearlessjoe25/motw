The Banyan Tribe

The Banyan Tribe is a lineage of soul-bound hunters, scholars, and warriors originating in West Africa. From Panin-Anasi I, the founder who united villages and crafted the first monster-based weapons, to later leaders like Asantewaa Banyan and Leygon Banyan, the family has harnessed the power of Legacy Weapons and spiritual gifts to defend their people and territory. Over centuries, they have faced rival clans, colonial conquest, and supernatural threats, preserving their knowledge, artifacts, and mystical techniques. Today, the Banyans continue their lineage of guardianship, blending ancient wisdom with modern strategies to protect their legacy and the chosen ones of the future.
