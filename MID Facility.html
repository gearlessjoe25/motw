Location Overview

Founded in 1902, the Monster Investigation Division (M.I.D.) operates as a covert government agency tasked with protecting humanity from supernatural entities seeking death, domination, or large-scale destruction. The Langley facility serves as the organization’s strategic nerve center.

Operations & Influence

Motive

The M.I.D. exists to safeguard humanity from monsters and occult forces that threaten civilian populations. While secrecy is paramount, the agency prioritizes containment over eradication—when possible.

Organizational Capabilities

Rapid Response Deployment

Elite teams can be dispatched within hours of supernatural detection, often arriving before local authorities are aware of the threat.

Information Suppression

The M.I.D. actively alters, removes, or obscures evidence to prevent public awareness of the supernatural.
