The Banyan Legacy

The Banyan family is an ancient lineage of monster hunters revered in West Africa long before the colonial era. For generations, they stood as guardians against the darkness, their name whispered with respect and fear in supernatural circles. However, when Anasi's father rejected the role of family head, the legacy began to fade from memory.

Anasi took on the responsibility himself, determined to restore the family's forgotten legacy.
